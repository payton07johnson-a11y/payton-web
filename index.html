About Me

Hey, I'm Payton.

I'm a web developer based in Gilmer, Texas who believes every local business deserves a website as unique as the people behind it. I don't use templates or page builders — I write every line of code by hand.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring East Texas, working on side projects, or helping small businesses figure out their online presence. I got into web development because I saw too many local businesses stuck with cookie-cutter websites that didn't represent them.

My mission is simple: give East Texas businesses a web presence they're proud of, at a price that makes sense.
